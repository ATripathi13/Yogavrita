## Getting Started

When you first open YOGAVRITA, you'll be greeted with a profile creation screen. This is your gateway to tracking your yoga journey.

## Creating Your Profile

### Required Information:
- **Name**: Your first name or preferred name
- **Email**: A valid email address for your profile

### Optional Settings:
- **Practice Time**: Set a daily reminder time (e.g., 7:00 AM)

### Steps:
1. Enter your name in the "Name" field
2. Enter your email in the "Email" field
3. (Optional) Select your preferred practice time
4. Click "Create Profile"

## Selecting a Class

After creating your profile, you'll see the home screen with six day cards (Monday through Saturday).

### Day Cards Show:
- **Day name**: The day of the week
- **"Today" badge**: Highlights the current day
- **Checkmark**: Shows if you've completed that day's practice this week

### To Start a Practice:
1. Click on any day card
2. The class sequence for that day will load
3. Your practice session begins automatically

### Notes:
- You can practice any day's sequence, not just today's
- Sunday is a rest day (no practice)
- Completing today's practice maintains your streak

- **Timer**: Countdown in MM:SS format

#### Breathing Indicator
- **Animated Circle**: Expands (inhale) and shrinks (exhale)
- **Breathing Pace**: Synced to the recommended cycle
- **Instructions**: "Inhale" and "Exhale" labels

## Understanding Streaks

### Current Streak
- Number of consecutive days you've practiced
- Resets to 0 if you miss a practice day
- Only counts Monday-Saturday (Sunday is rest day)

### Longest Streak
- Your personal best streak
- Never decreases
- Motivates you to beat your record

### How Streaks Work:
- Complete a class to increment your streak
- Practice must be on consecutive days (excluding Sunday)
- Saturday ‚Üí Monday counts as consecutive
- Missing any practice day (Mon-Sat) resets current streak
- You can only complete one session per day for streak purposes

## Tips for Best Experience

### For Your Practice:
1. **Find a quiet space** with enough room to move
2. **Use a yoga mat** for comfort and stability
3. **Wear comfortable clothing** that allows movement
4. **Stay hydrated** before and after practice
5. **Listen to your body** - use pause or skip if needed

### For the App:
1. **Use a larger screen** when possible for better visibility
2. **Enable sound** to hear timer notifications (if added)
3. **Bookmark the app** for quick access
4. **Practice at the same time** daily using the schedule feature
5. **Don't close the browser tab** during practice

### For Consistency:
1. **Set a daily reminder** in your profile
2. **Start with shorter sequences** if you're new
3. **Track your progress** by watching your streak grow
4. **Practice at the same time** each day
5. **Don't stress about perfection** - consistency matters more

### Browser Recommendations:
- **Chrome/Edge**: Best performance
- **Firefox**: Excellent compatibility
- **Safari**: Works well on Apple devices
- **Mobile**: Use landscape mode for practice

### Data Management:
- Your data is stored locally in your browser
- Clearing browser data will erase your profile
- No account needed - completely private
- Works offline once loaded

## Troubleshooting

### Timer not starting:
- Refresh the page
- Check browser console for errors
- Try a different browser

### Breathing indicator not animating:
- Ensure CSS animations are enabled
- Check browser compatibility
- Try disabling browser extensions

### Streak not updating:
- Ensure you completed the full session
- Check that you didn't exit early
- Verify the date/time is correct

### Profile not saving:
- Check browser LocalStorage is enabled
- Ensure you're not in private/incognito mode
- Try clearing browser cache
